Rules on the potting bench

Tap a gutter to rotate it clockwise. Right-click, long-press, or hold Shift while tapping to rotate backward.

  • 💧 is the downspout. Water follows matching open pipe ends.
  • 🌿 planters must all receive water.
  • 📦 evidence boxes must stay dry.
  • 🔒 locked gutters cannot be moved.
