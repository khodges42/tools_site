Spore Ledger 83

A playable ecosystem ledger. You do not control a hero. You interfere with a small generated island: seed moss, burn rot, dig channels, move stone, release beetles, and watch lineages adapt, starve, migrate, overbreed, or become local mythology.

island

Click/drag to paint the selected intervention. Space pauses. Each season changes rain, heat, mutations, food chains, and rumors in the ledger. Goal-ish: reach year 12 with at least three living species and no single organism owning the island.

interventions

Interventions cost attention. Attention returns each year. Over-interference makes the island brittle: mutation spikes, fires spread faster, and animals flee your cursor.
