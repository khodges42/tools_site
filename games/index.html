Games

A small shelf of static web games, toys, mistakes, and midnight experiments. Some are hand-built. Some are vibe coded. Some are generated with very little direction and then cleaned up later. Some are surprisingly good. Some may be broken in public.
Static only. These should run on Cloudflare Pages with plain files: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and whatever trouble a browser can make by itself.
Uneven on purpose. Treat this less like a polished arcade and more like a demoscene folder, a sketchbook, or a shoebox full of weird little disks.
Candle Courier A real-time wind-drawing game: guide a paper moth carrying one coal through a sleeping house, lighting candles while ceiling drips, vents, furniture, ember heat, and breath make every shove matter. wind Roof Cat Smuggler A real-time rooftop stealth run: steal moon-milk bottles, hide in chimneys, dodge sweeping porch lights, manage dash breath, and get back through the open window before the landlord follows your bell. stealth Moth Frequency A real-time shortwave tuning hunt: sweep the band, aim a roof-wire antenna, manage gain and battery, and copy five moth-coded broadcasts through weather ghosts and county appliance noise. radio Junk Drawer Saints A five-night placement-crafting score attack: turn buttons, bones, wire, wax, and rot into useful little miracles for impossible neighborhood requests before the drawer jams shut. crafting Tuna No Crust An SR20 engine tuning simulator: adjust fuel, ignition, boost, and supporting setup decisions while trying to make power without turning your lovely little Nissan heart into glitter. tuning Ghost Garage A choice-based S14 grief-and-tuning story: Lilith changes fluids, loads a basemap, buys questionable parts, meets another haunted car person, and tries to build something without needing permission from the dead. story/tune Borrowed Teeth Library A twelve-round occult lending-desk strategy game: buy cursed books, shelve them for tag adjacency bonuses, serve impossible patrons, and keep gossip from killing the library. strategy Interactive Fiction Library A text-adventure-ish library entry. Earlier experiment, possibly fancy, possibly haunted by the phrase “interactive fiction.” story Star-Marrow Municipal transcendence, cassette tape memory, abandoned timelines, and a future that died but left the lights on. rpg Tilt Reliquary A gyroscope maze ritual about rolling a witness through drifting walls, red bells, and a center aperture that only opens after seven seals. motion Tensor Altar A touch and gyroscope tesseract toy where you slice, unfold, and tap impossible rooms inside a projected 4D cube. 4d
No leaderboard. No accounts. No promise that anything here is finished. If something rules, great. If something breaks, that is also historically consistent with obscure personal websites.
