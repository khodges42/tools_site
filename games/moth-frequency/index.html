Notebook

The receiver finds illegal moth census stations. A strong signal needs close frequency, antenna angle, and enough gain. Too much gain hears every appliance in the county.

  • Drag the scope or use the tuning slider to sweep.
  • Use antenna angle to separate real broadcasts from weather ghosts.
  • Copy only when the needle holds steady and the code is readable.
The radio smells like warm dust. Find five broadcasts. The first one is usually hiding near a loud peak.
Mechanic: continuous signal hunting, not placement. The player learns to read peak width, directionality, gain noise, and drift under battery pressure. Every night rerolls real stations and decoys.
