Touch the shadow of a 4D thing until it admits which room is real.

GYROSCOPE / TOUCH / TESSERACT

Find rooms in the folded object.

This is not a physics simulation. It is a little machine for poking at a 4D cube's shadow. Tilt your phone to rotate the higher-dimensional camera. Drag to rotate the visible shadow. Pinch, mouse wheel, or press auto-unfold to open the tesseract into something uglier and more legible.

  • Gold node = current room to find.
  • Pink slice line = your current W-plane cross-section.
  • Green skeleton = projected tesseract edges.
  • Tap/click the gold room when you think you have it.

What the rules mean

A tesseract has sixteen corners. The game names each corner as a room using four signs, like +-++. You are not solving math homework; you are rotating a shadow, slicing it along W, and unfolding it until the named room feels obvious enough to touch.
