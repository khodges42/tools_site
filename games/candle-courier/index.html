Candle Courier

You are a paper moth carrying one stubborn coal through a sleeping house. Draw gusts with your finger or mouse. Light every candle before rain, dust, and bad angles put you out.

Matches found under the sink

Drag to draw a gust. The moth is light: short taps nudge, long strokes shove. Gusts cost breath; breath refills only when you stop bossing the air around.

  • 🕯 Touch dark candles to light them.
  • 💧 Avoid drips. They wound the ember.
  • 🪟 Blue vents push whether you like it or not.
  • ⬛ Furniture blocks the moth and breaks lazy straight lines.
First move: drag from behind the moth toward a candle. You are not flying the moth. You are arguing with the room.
A static Canvas game. No assets, no server, no leaderboard. The house is generated from a seed; the best room reached is saved in localStorage.
