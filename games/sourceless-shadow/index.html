Sourceless Shadow

The bulb has been stolen from the moon. Drag the wandering light around the roofs and use chimneys, laundry, and aerials to cast shadows over bright windows. Do not shade the cats; they know exactly who did it.

Rules of borrowed darkness

The repeated action is not movement through space; it is changing a projection. One light position redraws the whole city.

  • Drag/click/touch to move the yellow light.
  • Objects cast long shadows away from the light.
  • A lit window goes out after staying mostly covered.
  • Cats panic if shadow sits on them too long.
  • Later nights add moving cats and swaying laundry.
First thirty seconds: drag the light along the sky edge and watch which chimney shadow crosses the most windows without touching a cat.
