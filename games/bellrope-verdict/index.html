Rules of the hanging court

Cut cords, then let gravity testify. The red bead must land in the saucer. Bells are failure pressure, not collectibles.

  • Click/tap a cord to cut it.
  • Hold H or the hush button to dampen sway before committing.
  • You only get a few cuts. Some cords are load-bearing lies.
  • Ringing three bells spoils the verdict.
  • R restarts the same hanging. N makes a new one.
First thirty seconds: watch the mobile breathe, then cut a cord whose tension seems to be holding the witness away from the saucer.
A small static game about commitment under tension. The loop is observe a physical system → make irreversible cuts → read the consequences. No grid, no avatar route, no target imitation, no craft grade, no job queue.
