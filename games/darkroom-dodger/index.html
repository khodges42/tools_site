Darkroom notes

The repeated action is not movement or placement. You are shaping a print with light and chemistry.

  • Hold the lamp and drag on the paper to burn areas darker.
  • Move quickly over pale zones; linger over shadows that need weight.
  • D develops the latent exposure. Too little looks thin; too much blocks up.
  • S stops chemistry. F fixes and scores the print.
  • Hot lamps bloom wider. Tired developer loses contrast.
First thirty seconds: hold the lamp and scrub light onto the blank paper until its big shapes resemble the target. Then develop and fix.
