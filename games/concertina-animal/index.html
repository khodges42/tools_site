Rules of printed muscle

The repeated action is topological reversal. A fold does not move one token; it reverses a whole side, flips its faces, and stacks it onto another history.

  • Choose whether the left or right side folds.
  • Tap a perforation between panels to fold that side over the crease.
  • Folded panels flip to their back face and land on top.
  • Win with no visible teeth and at least three visible eyes.
  • Any stack thicker than four panels tears. Running out of folds also loses.
First thirty seconds: fold once near an edge and watch the symbols reverse. The back of a harmless moon may be a tooth.
