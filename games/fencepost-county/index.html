Fencepost County

A tiny procedural county RPG about seven days before the bus leaves. People keep schedules, carry grudges, repeat rumors, and remember favors. Fix the relay, steal from barns, mediate feuds, hunt coyotes, or just leave with a pocket full of somebody else's nails.

county map

Move with arrows/WASD/numpad or buttons. @ you, ☺ person, T town, R relay, M mine, ♣ woods, ≈ water, * shrine, ! trouble. Each step is one hour.

what you can do here

Tip: the map scrolls horizontally on tiny screens. The buttons are safer than diagonal keyboard gestures on mobile.
