My Take on an I Ching Tool

The I Ching is a beautiful tradition and a remarkable tool for reflection. I have tremendous respect for the culture that has preserved it for thousands of years, and this project is offered in that spirit of respect and curiosity.

This site is my personal interpretation and presentation of the I Ching. It combines traditional ideas with my own modern reflections and is intended as a companion for contemplation rather than an authoritative translation. I expect it to evolve over time as I continue learning, refining the writing, and experimenting with different visual styles and ways of presenting the material.

This is not my culture, and I was not born into this tradition. If I’ve misunderstood something, presented something inaccurately, or overlooked important context, I would genuinely appreciate hearing from people with more knowledge or lived experience. I built this because I admire the I Ching, and I’d love for it to become more thoughtful and more respectful with each iteration.

If this site sparks your curiosity, I encourage you to read the traditional sources as well. The I Ching has been interpreted for centuries, and no single website or translation can capture the full depth of the text.

## My Thoughts 

I utilize I Ching in my life as a mirror for reflection in the same way many use tarot. I don't personally use this for literal divination, predicting the future, things like that- although many do. I just find that an element of randomness is really interesting to incorporate into a thought process in the way that this system presents doing that is elegant and beautiful and very much in the same realm as my mental model of computer science at a high-level.
